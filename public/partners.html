Partnership Terms

Good to Know

  • Commission is paid after the client's monthly payment clears — no risk of chargeback.
  • Commission is calculated on the amount the client actually pays, including any plan discounts.
  • You can refer as many clients as you like — there is no cap on earnings.
  • Your commission runs for the full duration of the client's original contract (3, 6, or 12 months).
  • If the client renews, a new commission cycle starts from the renewal date.
  • There is no joining fee and no minimum referral requirement.
  • UrbanVault reserves the right to update commission rates with 30 days' notice to active partners.
